Larry's Lovely Lasagna

What is this recipe?

Larry's Lovely Lasagna is the best lasagna ever made. It has saved countless lives and won multiple awards. Let's check it out!

Ingredients

  • pears, tears of your firstborn, cheese, lettuce, rice, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Take pears out of your oven.
  2. Throw cheese and tears onto the floor
  3. Shred lettuce and season with salt and pepper
  4. Now set it all on fire until crisp
  5. Congrats! You've made Larry's Lovely Lasagna! I hope you enjoy the world changing taste of this recipe. We are not liable for any damages.
