Bob's Baked Cake

Whats the recipe?

Bob's Baked Cake is a fantastic dessert, and is served in Gordon Ramsey's restaurants! While Gordon hasn't specifically named it, we assure you you can find this recipe at all of the famous high-dining locations.

Ingredients

  • Cake, bought from a supermarket

Steps

  1. Buy cake from supermarket
  2. Throw cake in oven, bake for 30 mins at 400F
  3. Nice! Its all finished. Enjoy and serve to all your friends, make sure to recommend
